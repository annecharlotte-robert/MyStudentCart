About Me

Why did you do Ironhack?

During my seven years as a graphic designer, I had the possibility to work on several user interfaces, what I really enjoy but I never really learned to focus on the user. Ironhack allowed me to discover and learn the UX skills I was missing, and reinforce my UI design skills.

What do you look for in a company culture?

I'd like to work in a company where the individual is respected and can grow and learn within a benevolent team, where failing can be a step to success.

Superpower & why?

My ability to adapt and my resistance to stress allowed me to cope with unexpected situations and carry projects to their ends.
